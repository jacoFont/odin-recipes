Gnocchi sorrentina

Description

A traditional italian dish, complete with gnocchi and tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • Gnocchi
  • Passata
  • Mozzarella
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil
  2. In a large frying pan, add 2 passatas and leave it in the pan to warm up
  3. Once warmed up, add some salt and pepper and the herbs
  4. Leave it on lowest heat and stir it occasionally
  5. While it is simmering, dice the mozzarella into very fine cubes
  6. Add the gnocchi in the boiling water
  7. Turn off the heat for the passata and in a rectangular glass dish, put a few spoons until it covers the base
  8. When the gnocchi is cooked, pick it up with the pasta strainer spoon and put it evenly over the passata base
  9. Take a handfull of mozzarella and sprinkle it over the gnocchi
  10. Add a few more spoons of passata to cover the gnocchi and the mozzarella and repeat until there is no more gnocchi left, making sure there is some
    passata and mozzarella left over
  11. Use all the passata and mozzarella to cover the remaining layer of gnocchi
  12. Put it in the pre-heated oven and leave for about 10 minutes
  13. Dish out and serve
