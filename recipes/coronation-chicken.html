Coronation Chicken

Description

Diced potatoes melting in a traditional curry sauce with chicken pieces

Ingredients

  • Diced potatoes
  • Diced chicken
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mango chutney
  • Mild curry powder
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil
  2. Add the diced potatoes
  3. While the potatoes are cooking, dice the chicken and put to the side
  4. In a mixing bowl, put one bottle of mayonnaise, 2 tablespoons of mild curry powder and 1 tablesoon of mango chutney.
    Mix until a paste is formed
  5. When the sauce is done, start frying the chicken
  6. When the chicken is done, remove the excess liquid and leave back in the pan
  7. Drain the potatoes. Return to the pot and put in the cooked chicken, mixing well
  8. Add the curry sauce and mixing until everything is evenly distributed
  9. Serve and eat piping hot
