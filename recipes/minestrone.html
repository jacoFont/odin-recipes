Coronation Chicken

Description

Cooked vegetables and chicken along with pasta, mixed
together in a stock-based sauce

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Chicken
  • Pastina
  • Broccoli
  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable stock
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil with two stock cubes
  2. Add the diced potatoes
  3. While the potatoes are cooking, dice the carrots, broccoli, cabbage, chicken and put to the side
  4. Add the carrots, celery and broccoli into the same pan
  5. While this is cooking, start frying the diced chicken
  6. When the chicken is done, remove the excess liquid and put it in the pot with the vegetables
  7. Bring another pot of salted water to the boil
  8. Leave everything to simmer, checking once in a while
  9. When the salted water is boiling, add the pastina
  10. When the pasta is done, drain it and leave it in the drainer
  11. With a potato masher, mash the pot of vegetables until it resembles a thick mixture with a few lumps in it
  12. Add in the drained pasta, season it, stir it and serve
